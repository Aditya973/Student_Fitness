Steps

1- Stand on the edge of a step. Or, if you have a step-aerobics platform, place two sets of risers underneath the platform.
2- Stand tall with your abdominals pulled in, the balls of your feet firmly planted on the step, and your heels hanging over the edge. Rest your hands against a wall or a sturdy object for balance.

4- Hold the position for a moment, and then lower your heels below the platform, feeling a stretch in your calf muscles.
